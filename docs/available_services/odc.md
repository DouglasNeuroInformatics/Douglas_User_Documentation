# Open Data Capture
Open Data Capture (https://opendatacapture.org/) is the platform of choice for the collection of clinical research instruments, ranging from simple surveys to complex interactive testing.  Platform Features include:

## Multi-Modal Administration:
Conduct questionnaires and interactive tasks both in-person (via clinical workstations) and remotely (via a secure participant gateway).

## Anonymity and Longitudinal Tracking:
Utilize hash-based identification to follow patient trajectories over time without storing personal demographic data.

## Instrument Flexibility:
Access an ever-growing library of implemented instruments or develop bespoke ones using the flexible JSON and JavaScript-based build system.

## Secure Data Export:
Export your collected data at any time in CSV or TSV formats for analysis in external statistical software.

## Research Access:
The paid platform is available to any Douglas researcher upon request. A public demo is available for evaluation at https://demo.opendatacapture.org
